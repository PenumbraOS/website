## This Website

This site serves as the central resource for everything related to the Ai Pin in the post-Humane era. It contains:

- **PenumbraOS documentation** -- installation guides, usage docs, and developer resources for the custom firmware
- **Device knowledge** -- hardware and software documentation gathered through reverse engineering and community research
- **Archived content** -- official Humane support articles, product images, and press assets preserved before the original site went offline
